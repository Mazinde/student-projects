Our History

Mandera Girls Secondary School is located in Mandera Ward within Chalinze District, Pwani Region, Tanzania. The school is a government -run institution exclusively for girls, dedicated to empowering young women through quality education.

The school was officially registered on 7 January 2009, under registration number S.3996. It offers education from Form I to Form IV (O-Level), providing hostel facilities to accommodate students from diverse locations.

Mandera Girls Secondary School holds the NECTA registration number S4159 and is well equipped with essential facilities, including science laboratories,computer room, classrooms, a football field, and a library.

Community Support & Development

Mandera Ward, where the school is located, has a population of approximately 9,365 (2012 census), reflecting a vibrant community that supports educational development.
