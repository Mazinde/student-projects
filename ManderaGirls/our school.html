About Our School

Mandera Girls Secondary School is a government girls' school located in Chalinze, Pwani Region. We strive to nurture knowledge, skills, and character — with a focus on ICT, sciences, languages, and arts. Our environment encourages creativity, leadership, and community service.

  • 🎓Experienced, professionally registered teachers.
  • 🧪Equipped Science Laboratory and ICT Room.
  • 🏐Sports facilities and vibrant clubs.
  • 🛏️Safe hostels with a supportive learning atmosphere.
