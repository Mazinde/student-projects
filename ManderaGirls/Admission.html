Admission Requirements

We welcome all eligible girls who are passionate about learning and growth. Below are the general requirements for joining Mandera Girls Secondary School:

  • A copy of Birth Certificate
  • Recent passport-size photographs (3 pieces)
  • Transfer letter (for transfered students)
  • Medical checkup Report

Joining Instructions

Please read the instructions below carefully before reporting:

  1. Report to school on the official reporting date indicated in the admission letter.
  2. Come with all required documents and personal items as listed in the admission package.
  3. Students are expected to be in full school uniform on the reporting day.
  4. Parents/Guardians must accompany the student for orientation.
  5. Medical examination form must be duly filled by a certified doctor.
