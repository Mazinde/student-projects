About Study Nest

Study Nest is an educational platform created to support Tanzanian students with resources aligned to the new national curriculum. Our mission is to make learning more accessible, engaging, and effective.

We provide notes, textbooks and past papers for Forms 1 to 4 across all major subjects. Our goal is to empower learners with reliable study materials to prepare effectively for NECTA examinations and succeed academically.

Whether you're studying English, Kiswahili, Sciences, or Humanities, Study Nest is here to simplify your revision journey. Together, we believe every student can achieve success with the right tools and guidance.
